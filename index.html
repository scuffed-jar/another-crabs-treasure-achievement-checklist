Achievement Checklist

Warning: Contains Spoilers!

There are no missable achievements in this game, so to save time we are going to skip area coverage.

Checklist

  • Story Bosses
  • Side Bosses
  • Miscellaneous
  • Fork Upgrades
  • Shells

Story Bosses

  • Defeat Nephro. (Captain of the Guard)
  • Defeat a miniboss. (Polluted Platoon Pathfinder)
  • Defeat Magista. (Tyrant of Slacktide)
  • Defeat the Diseased Lichenthrope. (Diseased Lichenthrope)
  • Defeat Heikea. (Intimidation Crab)
  • Defeat the Ceviche Sisters. (Ceviche Sisters)
  • Defeat Voltai. (The Accumulator)
  • Defeat Pagurus. (The Ravenous)
  • Defeat Roland. (Venture Crabitalist)
  • Defeat Petroch. (The False Moon)
  • Defeat Inkerton. (Crab Hunter)
  • Defeat Camtscha. (The Bleached King)
  • Defeat Camtscha (For real). (Camtscha, Reborn)
  • Defeat Praya Dubia. (The Ocean's Agony)
  • Defeat Firth. (Avatar of Waste)

Side Bosses

  • Defeat the Royal Shellsplitter, who is located Southeast in the Shallow. (Royal Shellsplitter)
  • Defeat the Curdled Carbonara Connoisseur, who is hidden behind the gank waterfall north of the shopping cart, roughly in the center of the Expired Grove. (Curdled Carbonara Connoisseur)
  • Defeat Topoda. From Heikea's boss area (at Expired Grove -> Village Gates), go east, drop down, make your way through the cave to the Curdled Village. From there, fish hook your way up along the northern border, go all the way north, make a westward U-turn and go southwest towards the algae field.(Grovekeeper)
  • Defeat the Consortium. Can be fought in the northwestern area in Flotsam Vale. This area is intended to be accessed from the tree top path in the Expired Grove. Though you can just start at Flotsam Vale -> Company Town, walk all the way north and jump up to him, especially with Bartholomew's upwards boost shell ability, which is bought at Shellfish Desires in New Carcinia's Upper Crust.(The Consortium)
  • Defeat the Scuttling Sludge Steamroller, who can be fought in the middle of the wooden circle establishment in the eastern part of Flotsam Vale. (Scuttling Sludge Steamroller)

Miscellaneous

  • Start the treasure hunt. This is a story progression achievement and unmissable. (The Hunt Begins!)
  • Get your shell back. This is a story progression achievement and unmissable. (Finishing What I Started)
  • Fall off a cliff. Can be done anywhere. (You fell off)
  • Purchase some insurance. Get to New Carcinia -> Upper Crust and go all the way Northwest to buy Shell insurance at Shellfish Desires. (In Good Claws)
  • Max out all 3 skill branches. Unlocking all of them at once requires 660 out of 1400 of the purple crystals. You can also unlock them one by one with just 235 crystals, by resetting with Shark Eggs after each branch (most easily obtained in New Carcinia - Lower Crust at the Prawn shop).
    • Unlock all skills on the Path of The True Crab. (Path of the True Crab)
    • Unlock all skills on the Path of The Hermit. (Path of the Hermit)
    • Unlock all skills on the Path of The Predator. (Path of the Predator)
  • Shoot a crab with a gun. Go to Settings -> Assist mode and toggle the Gun option. This does NOT work from the main menu. You need to load into the actual game for the option to become available. Once that's done, find a crab and shoot it.(This Kills The Crab)
  • Find a certain hero's helmet. Start at The Old Ocean -> Lookout Tower. Make your way east towards the building with the huge elevator. Upon leaving the elevator on the upper floor, hug the left-hand wall, the one initially leading South. Around the corner, there's gonna be a cracked wall leading to broken stairs going upwards. This leads to a pretty lengthy Jump & Run section, which ends on an island with the Helmet in the middle. (Shellslike)
  • Learn all adaptations. There's 8 adaptations in the game. (Well Adapted)
    • 6 of them get unlocked upon killing bosses (Magista, Topoda, Consortium, Cevice Sisters, Voltai, Petroch).
    • The 7th adaptation can be obtained in New Carcinia -> Lower Crust. Find Erich, the talking Urchin, and keep "hugging" him.
    • The 8th adaptation is in the Sands between. Go all the way west and to the top of the spiked hill, next to the garbage dumpster.

Fork Upgrades

(Make It Shine)

  • New Carcinia - Reef's Edge: Backtrack to the grappling hook platform.
  • New Carcinia - Lower Crust: Buy at Prawn Shop for 5000 Microplastics.
  • Expired Grove: Defeat Heikea.
  • Expired Grove: Go all the way north on the path between Topoda and the Curdled Village. There's gonna be a hole dropping you down into a cave. Open the shell inside.
  • Flotsam Vale - Factory: After the first Magnet, it's at the end of the fishing net Jump & Run.
  • Flotsam Vale - Factory: From the roof of the building with the Mag-Rail Platform fast travel point, complete another fishing net Jump & Run
  • Flotsam Vale - Factory: Make your way to the roof with the circling magnet. The Relic is downstairs.
  • The Sands Between (after having obtained Topoda's and Voltai's adaptations): Go to the upper level of the eastern region by smashing an Umami Cube. The relic's northwest of the broom.
  • The Sands Between (after having obtained Topoda's and Voltai's adaptations): Go to the upper level of the northwestern region by smashing another Umami Cube and getting access to a wind shaft. Go up and find the 2 crabs fighting with miniature ships and bottles. One of them drops the relic.
  • The Sands Between (after having obtained Topoda's and Voltai's adaptations): From the previous relic, go north to find a battery-crab. Activate it and follow the path for another relic.
  • The Sands Between (after having obtained Topoda's and Voltai's adaptations): From the fast travel point just before Flotsam Vale, go south for another battery crab. The relic is on the bridge.
  • The Unfathom - Hermit's Plateau: Go east and complete the short sponge Jump & Run.
  • The Unfathom - Luminous Respite: Go to the area northwest with the giant crabs. Relic is somewhere in the middle..
  • The Unfathom - Defeat Inkerton..
  • The Old Ocean - City Gates: Progress normally to the building with a shortcut on the roof. The one with a dangling chain next to it, where you talked to Chitan. From the rooftop look directly south and you'll see the shell with the relic, on a platform reached by jumping on a blue sponge.
  • (Bonus) The Old Ocean - Lookout Tower: Just north of the Moon Snail Shell is a blue sponge semi-hidden beneath the ledge. Jump on that sponge and the one almost immediately after it to get to a rooftop path. Final Relic is at the southern end of it.
